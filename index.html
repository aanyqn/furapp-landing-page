Why we build this furAPP

We built furAPP around one simple idea: friendship shouldn't be this hard. Most apps are designed to keep you scrolling, not connecting. furAPP flips that. It surfaces people near you who are genuinely open to meeting someone new — no pressure, no awkward cold messages, just shared interests and a nudge in the right direction. From casual hangouts to long-term friendships, furAPP is where it starts.
